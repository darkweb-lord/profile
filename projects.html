Body control Automation Using CAN

Implemented CAN protocol for efficient, reliable communication with fault recovery and noise reduction in vehicle body functions.

Problem: Traditional vehicle body functions (indicators, wipers) face wiring complexity and communication issues.

Solution: CAN protocol for efficient communication with fault recovery.

Implementation: Used four ARM-based boards with sensors, actuators, and CAN transceivers.

Automatic Coconut Farm Land Maintenance Rover with Precision Mapping

An autonomous rover automates weeding and herbicide spraying, reducing labor and chemical exposure.

Problem: Manual coconut farming relies on labor, health risks, and inefficient resource use.

Solution: Autonomous rover automates tasks, minimizing chemical exposure and optimizing resource usage.

Implementation: Equipped with ultrasonic sensors, a microcontroller-based motor system, and a 16Ah battery.

Automation on pulp tank monitoring and water Jet cutting system

Automated system using an Arduino Uno for real-time monitoring and precise level control.

Problem: Manual monitoring of pulp levels and water jet cutting causes inefficiencies.

Solution: Automated system for real-time monitoring and optimized cutting.

Implementation: Ultrasonic sensors detect pulp levels, Arduino controls the motor, and an automated water jet cutter ensures accuracy.
